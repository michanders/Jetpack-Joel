Jetpack Joel

SPACEBAR or CLICK to Start

Welcome To Jetpack Joel! After abandoning his ship due to power loss, Joel is advised to get as close to the rendezvous point as possible. Use SPACEBAR or CLICK to help Joel navigate the increasingly dangerous asteroid fields! Press "B" or 2-finger screen touch to deploy J-BOMB 1650's. Keep an eye on your fuel meter. Fuel tanks only appear every so often... Good luck!
